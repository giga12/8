Description

OxGEn Molecular PCR Fragment Purification Kit is simple system for high quality PCR fragments purification using a rapid spin column protocol. Kit contains sufficient materials for 50 purification. The purified PCR fragments is of excellent quality and yield and completely suitable with following applications including: Sequencing, Southern Blot analysis and more. The protocol can be completed including incubation time in approximately 15 minutes.
